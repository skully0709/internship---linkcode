The Story of Rolex

Founded in 1905 by Hans Wilsdorf, Rolex has grown to become the most recognizable luxury watch brand in the world. Headquartered in Geneva, Switzerland, the company is legendary for creating robust, highly reliable, and timeless designs that retain value over decades.

Rolex pioneered several major milestones in watchmaking history, including the creation of the first waterproof wristwatch case (The Oyster) in 1926 and the first automatic self-winding mechanism.
