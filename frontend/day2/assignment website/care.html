Preserving Your Timepiece

A well-maintained mechanical or quartz watch can last for generations. Regular upkeep not only keeps the timekeeping accurate but also preserves the structural integrity and finish of the case and strap over time.

Below is a straightforward guide detailing daily habits, professional servicing schedules, and proper storage standards for your collection.

Daily Maintenance Guide

Focus Area Best Practice Instruction
Cleaning Wipe down the dial glass and strap daily with a clean, dry microfiber cloth.
Magnetism Avoid placing your watch directly near strong magnets, speakers, or tablets.
Servicing Mechanical automatic movements should be oiled and checked every 3 to 5 years.
